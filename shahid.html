Government Freedom Struggle Museum (Shahid Smarak)

History

Martyr Smarak is one of the oldest places in Meerut which was built to commemorate the revolt of 1857. Situated near the Company Gardens, this monument hosts some important events during the main national holidays of India. Shaheed Smarak also houses the government-run Freedom Struggle Museum which came into existence in 1997. The main objective of the museum is the collection, conservation, documentation and exhibition of cultural assets and making it available for educational activities as well as to create awareness about our glorious past. There are two galleries within the museum containing paintings and artifacts from the time.

Interesting Facts for tourists to visit

  • It is a place to pay respect to the freedom fighters who have sacrificed their lives for our country.
  • All the artifacts in the museum are well preserved and the galleries provide a perfect pictorial representation of the freedom struggle.

Time to Visit

Best Time to Visit: Monday to Sunday from 6:00 AM to 8:30 PM.
