Gandhi Bagh

History

Gandhi Bagh is located on the Mall Road and is one of the oldest parks in Meerut which was established by the East India Company. The park has a 1025 meter walking track, beautiful Botanical Garden, more than 100 years old banyan tree, a cricket ground, musical fountain and a children's park. This park is maintained by Meerut Cantonment Board. It has a canteen for refreshments and an army canteen for good snacks and meals.

Interesting Facts for tourists to visit

  • This is the oldest and largest garden of Meerut city.
  • This place is a paradise for morning walkers
  • Gandhi Bagh is controlled by the Indian Army Cantonment Board. It has a wide array of seasonal and permanent flowering plants, and some old trees such as the banyan tree.
  • The park has lots of slides, swings, monkey crawls etc. for the children to play.
  • It has a water tank which is more than a century old, which was brought from Delhi long ago by the British.

Time to Visit

Gandhi Bagh open from 06:00 am to 08:00 pm, all days in week.
