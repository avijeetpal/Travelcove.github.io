Suraj Kund Temple

History

The Suraj Kund temple was built in 1714 by a wealthy merchant, Lavar Jawahar Lal. The main attraction near this temple is an earthen its source is river Ganges. You can visit Baba Manohar Nath Temple and Mansa Devi Temple and many other smaller temples which are within the Suraj Kund Temple. Suraj Kund Temple attracts a lot of tourists and locals visit this temple complex very often. This place has history from the times of Mahabharata; it is believed that King Karna devoted his ‘kawach and kundal’ (divine armor) to Sun God at this place.

Interesting Facts for tourists to visit

  • The green ambience of this place is a major attraction for the visitors.
  • It offers many recreational and amusement activities for the tourists and visitors.
  • The King Karna devoted his ‘kawach and kundal’ (divine armor) to Sun God at this place.
  • Every year, a fair is organised during the time of Dussehra, which is celebrated with full zeal and enthusiasm.

Time to Visit

Suraj Kund Temple open from 05:00 am to 09:00 pm, all days in week, No entry fee.
